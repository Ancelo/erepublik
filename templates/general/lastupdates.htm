Day 1,767

Removal of Residence building

Starting today, the Residence building (former Town center) is being removed.

If you previously made upgrades to your Residence using currency, you will find the compensation in your storage for your expense. In addition, from now on, each level up will reward you with a full refill of energy.

The maximum energy for a new citizen is 100 and will increase with 20 at every level up until level 21. At level 21 a citizen will have a maximum energy of at least 500. After level 21, the maximum energy can be increased by purchasing an Energy Center.

Eg:

At level 1 the citizen has a maximum energy of 100.
At level 2 the citizen has a maximum energy of 120.
At level 11 the citizen has a maximum energy of 300.
At level 12 the citizen has a maximum energy of 320.

The citizens that did not have a quality 5 Residence and have a minimum level of 21 will get an increase of their maximum energy to 500.

The citizens that are under level 21, will receive an increase of their maximum energy depending on their level.

The citizens that already had a quality Residence will have the same maximum energy they had until now.

Day 1,757

Training Contract

There are 2 types of Training Contracts available on the Special Item page:

1. With 30 Gold you receive 50% discount on training. This discount is applied during a 30 days period, for all the buildings you choose to use

Ex : if you train with all the 4 buildings you pay 3.47 Gold daily. By having this contract you will pay only 1.735 Gold daily

2. With 65 Gold you receive 90% discount on training. This discount is applied during a 30 days period, for all the buildings you choose to use.

Ex : if you train with all the 4 buildings you pay 3.47 Gold daily. By having this contract you will pay only 0.347 Gold daily

You cannot sign both contracts so you need to choose which one is more suitable for your needs.

You can only sign the contract once, thus you cannot have the discount more than 30 days .

On the Training page you will be able to see the remaining days of contract availability.

Day 1,752

Mass destruction weapons : BOMBS and ROCKETS

Until the end of Day 1,755 win Rockets for every battle hero medal and Bombs (5,000,000 damage) for every campaign hero medal! Use them to win your battles!

The Rockets damage varies according to the division you are in:

Div 1 : 75.000 damage

Div 2 : 375.000 damage

Div 3 : 750.000 damage

Div 4 : 1.500.000 damage

This damage is counted only for influence bar and your battle damage. No rank points or experience points will be added for using these mass destruction weapons!

The weapons can be used in a period of 30 days , after they will expire (Day 1786).

Check your Storage to see how many weapons you have available and when is the last day you can use them. The mass destruction weapons have a separate storage slot than the Rockets you can produce each day.

Check your profile page after you used one of the weapons to see their history : when you used it and in which battle.

Day 1,744

Health to Energy

Beginning on day 1743, the concept of “Health” is renamed to “Energy” . Working, training and fighting consume the same amount of Energy as they did with Health before. Likewise, Food will recover an equal amount of Energy. Apart from the name change, the following aspects of the game are modified:

- Your maximum recoverable Energy will be instantly available . Example: If you have a Q3 Town Center, the former Health Bar will no longer show 100 Health, but 300 Energy.

- Same as before, if you have a Q3 Town Center, you will be able to recover a maximum of 300 Energy at one time . This means that if you start a gaming session at full Energy you can play nearly twice as long as before. Example: Before you had 100 Health + 300 recoverable Health. Now you have 300 Energy + 300 recoverable Energy

- Speaking of Town Center, it will be renamed to “Residence”

- You will be able to upgrade your Residence by clicking the button conveniently placed next to your Energy indicator

- Finally, the rule for recovering Energy is slightly changed: instead of being able to recover 100 Energy per hour, you will be able to recover 10 Energy every 6 minutes.
